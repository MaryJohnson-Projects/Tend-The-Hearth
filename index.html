Tend the Hearth

A system agnostic TTRPG supplement

In Tend the Hearth your characters have ventured into the wilderness towards an abandoned structure. Your goal is to reconstruct it into a safe haven for you and your group. Unfortunately, it was abandoned for a reason, and it is entirely possible you will meet the same fate.
